## SECTION 1: INTRODUCTION AND PURPOSE

This Delivery and Shipping Policy establishes comprehensive guidelines for the delivery of products purchased from TechMart Electronics Retailer to customers across the Arab Republic of Egypt. The purpose of this policy is to ensure timely, secure, and reliable delivery services while maintaining transparency in shipping procedures, costs, and customer responsibilities throughout the delivery process.

TechMart is committed to providing exceptional delivery experiences that meet or exceed customer expectations. We understand that prompt and careful delivery is a critical component of customer satisfaction and overall shopping experience. This policy outlines our delivery standards, shipping methodologies, geographic coverage, timeframes, tracking mechanisms, and procedures for handling delivery-related issues including delays, damages, and failed delivery attempts.

This document serves as the official framework for all delivery and shipping operations conducted by TechMart and its authorized courier partners. All TechMart logistics personnel, warehouse staff, courier partners, and customer service representatives must adhere to the provisions set forth in this policy. Customers are encouraged to review this policy carefully to understand their rights, responsibilities, and available options regarding product delivery. This policy complies with Egyptian Commercial Law, Consumer Protection Law No. 181 of 2018, and industry best practices for e-commerce logistics operations.

## SECTION 3: GENERAL TERMS AND CONDITIONS

### Article 3.1: Delivery Service Standards

**3.1.1 Delivery Commitment:** TechMart guarantees delivery of products within the timeframes specified in Section 5, calculated from the order confirmation timestamp. Delivery times begin counting from the moment the order status changes to "Shipped" in the customer's account portal.

**3.1.2 Professional Handling:** All products are handled by trained logistics personnel and transported using appropriate vehicles equipped with proper securing mechanisms to prevent damage during transit. Fragile items receive additional protective packaging and are marked clearly on all sides.

**3.1.3 Tracking Transparency:** Every shipment is assigned a unique tracking number that allows customers to monitor their order's progress in real-time through multiple channels including SMS updates, email notifications, mobile app tracking, and website portal.

**3.1.4 Delivery Confirmation:** Upon successful delivery, customers must sign a delivery receipt confirming receipt of the package in good condition. For contactless delivery (when requested), photographic evidence of delivery location is captured and shared with the customer.

**3.1.5 Package Inspection Rights:** Customers have the right to inspect the exterior packaging before accepting delivery. If packaging shows signs of damage, tampering, or mishandling, customers may refuse delivery or note the damage on the delivery receipt before accepting the package.

### Article 3.2: Shipping Cost Structure

TechMart employs a transparent pricing model for delivery services based on order value, product category, delivery zone, and service level selected:

**3.2.1 Free Shipping Eligibility:**
- Orders with total value of 1,000 EGP or more: Free standard delivery to Zones 1, 2, and 3
- Orders with total value of 2,000 EGP or more: Free standard delivery to all zones (Zones 1-5)
- VIP members (Gold, Platinum, Diamond): Free delivery on all orders regardless of value
- Promotional periods: As announced on website and marketing channels

**3.2.2 Standard Shipping Fees (Orders Below Free Shipping Threshold):**

| Delivery Zone | Order Value < 500 EGP | Order Value 500-999 EGP |
|---------------|----------------------|------------------------|
| Zone 1 (Cairo/Giza) | 50 EGP | 30 EGP |
| Zone 2 (Alexandria, Major Cities) | 75 EGP | 50 EGP |
| Zone 3 (Delta Governorates) | 100 EGP | 75 EGP |
| Zone 4 (Upper Egypt) | 150 EGP | 100 EGP |
| Zone 5 (Coastal/Remote) | 200 EGP | 150 EGP |

**3.2.3 Express Delivery Fees (Additional Cost):**
- Zone 1: +50 EGP (12-24 hour delivery)
- Zone 2: +75 EGP (24-36 hour delivery)
- Zone 3: +100 EGP (48-hour delivery)
- Zones 4-5: Express delivery not available

**3.2.4 Special Handling Fees:**
- Large appliances requiring two-person delivery team: +100 EGP
- Products requiring professional installation: Installation fee ranges from 200-800 EGP depending on product complexity (quoted separately)
- Weekend delivery (Friday/Saturday): +50 EGP surcharge
- Evening delivery after 6 PM: +30 EGP surcharge
- Redelivery attempts after failed delivery: 50 EGP per additional attempt (after 3rd attempt)

### Article 3.3: Delivery Address Requirements

**3.3.1 Complete Address Information:** Customers must provide accurate and complete delivery addresses including:
- Full street name and building number
- Floor and apartment/office number
- Nearest landmark for easy identification
- Area/district name
- City and governorate
- Alternative phone number for delivery coordination

**3.3.2 Address Verification:** TechMart reserves the right to contact customers to verify delivery addresses before dispatch. Incorrect or incomplete addresses that result in delivery delays or failures are the customer's responsibility.

**3.3.3 Address Changes:** Customers may request address changes up to 24 hours after order placement for standard delivery, or up to 12 hours for express delivery. Address changes after the cutoff time may not be possible if the order has already been dispatched. Change requests must be submitted through customer service at 16555.

**3.3.4 Undeliverable Addresses:** TechMart does not deliver to P.O. Box addresses, temporary locations, or areas deemed unsafe by courier partners. If an address is identified as undeliverable, the customer will be contacted to provide an alternative address or arrange pickup from the nearest TechMart retail location.

### Article 3.4: Delivery Recipient Requirements

**3.4.1 Authorized Recipients:** Products may be received by:
- The customer who placed the order (preferred)
- Adult household members (18 years or older) at the delivery address
- Authorized representative with written authorization and valid ID
- Building security/reception (only if customer pre-authorizes via account settings or phone call)

**3.4.2 Age-Restricted Products:** Certain products may require delivery to adults only (18+). Valid government-issued identification must be presented upon delivery for age verification.

**3.4.3 Delivery Refusal Rights:** Recipients have the right to refuse delivery if:
- Packaging shows visible damage, tampering, or water damage
- Product does not match the order description on the delivery note
- Delivery occurs outside the agreed time window without prior notice
- Courier cannot provide proper identification

**3.4.4 Cash on Delivery (COD) Payment:** For COD orders, payment must be made in Egyptian Pounds (EGP) at the time of delivery. Couriers carry change for bills up to 500 EGP. Exact change is appreciated for larger amounts. Credit card payment on delivery is not available.

### Article 3.5: Delivery Attempts and Failed Deliveries

**3.5.1 Maximum Delivery Attempts:** TechMart will make up to three (3) delivery attempts at no additional charge:
- First attempt: As per scheduled delivery timeframe
- Second attempt: Next business day, customer contacted to arrange convenient time
- Third attempt: Within 2 business days of second attempt, customer must confirm availability

**3.5.2 Failed Delivery Scenarios:**
- Customer not available at delivery address
- Customer refuses to accept delivery
- Delivery address incorrect or inaccessible
- Customer does not respond to courier's calls (minimum 3 call attempts)
- Unsafe delivery conditions (severe weather, security concerns)

**3.5.3 After Three Failed Attempts:**
- Order held at TechMart returns center for 7 calendar days
- Customer notified via SMS and email to arrange pickup or provide alternative delivery address
- Redelivery fee of 50 EGP applies for 4th attempt or beyond
- After 7 days, if customer does not respond, order is automatically cancelled and refund processed minus 5% administrative fee and any incurred shipping costs

**3.5.4 Customer Responsibilities:** Customers must ensure availability at the delivery address during the scheduled delivery window or provide accurate contact information for delivery coordination. Repeated failed deliveries due to customer unavailability may result in restrictions on future COD orders.

### Article 3.6: Product Inspection Upon Delivery

**3.6.1 External Inspection:** Customers should inspect the exterior packaging for any signs of damage before accepting delivery. Look for:
- Crushed or torn boxes
- Water damage or stains
- Opened or resealed packaging
- Missing security seals or tape

**3.6.2 Documentation of Damage:** If damage is observed:
- Note the damage clearly on the delivery receipt before signing
- Take photographs of damaged packaging from multiple angles
- Request the courier to document the damage in their system
- Contact TechMart customer service within 24 hours at 16555

**3.6.3 Contents Verification:** While complete unboxing and testing is not required at delivery, customers should verify:
- Number of packages matches the order (for multi-box orders)
- Product model visible on packaging matches the order
- All listed accessories and components are present (based on box labeling)

**3.6.4 Concealed Damage:** Damage not visible during delivery inspection but discovered within 48 hours of delivery should be reported immediately to customer service with photographic evidence. TechMart will investigate and process claims under the Return Policy (TM-POL-001-2024) or Warranty Policy (TM-POL-004-2024) as applicable.

### Article 3.7: Courier Partner Standards

**3.7.1 Authorized Courier Partners:** TechMart works exclusively with licensed and insured courier companies that meet our quality and reliability standards:
- Egypt Post Express
- Aramex Egypt
- DHL Express Egypt
- Fedex Egypt
- TechMart proprietary delivery fleet (Cairo and Giza only)

**3.7.2 Courier Identification:** All delivery personnel must:
- Wear company uniforms or identification badges
- Carry official identification cards
- Use branded delivery vehicles with company logos
- Provide tracking number upon request

**3.7.3 Professional Conduct:** Couriers are required to:
- Treat customers with respect and courtesy
- Handle products with care and professionalism
- Respect customer privacy and property
- Follow all delivery instructions provided by customers
- Not request tips or gratuities (voluntary only)

**3.7.4 Customer Safety:** Customers should never allow delivery personnel inside their homes unless it's for professional installation services pre-arranged with TechMart. All standard deliveries should be received at the door/entrance.

### Article 3.8: Legal Compliance

This policy is subject to the laws of the Arab Republic of Egypt, including but not limited to:
- Consumer Protection Law No. 181 of 2018
- Egyptian Commercial Law provisions regarding sales and delivery
- Electronic Commerce Law regarding online transactions
- Transportation and logistics regulations
- Customs regulations for imported products

In case of any dispute, the provisions of Egyptian law shall prevail, and disputes shall be resolved through the Consumer Protection Agency or competent Egyptian courts.

## SECTION 4: STEP-BY-STEP DELIVERY PROCEDURES

### STEP 1: Order Placement and Confirmation

**Action:** Customer completes purchase through TechMart website, mobile app, or phone order.

**Order Confirmation Process:**
- Customer receives immediate on-screen order confirmation with order number
- Confirmation email sent to registered email address within 5 minutes
- Confirmation SMS sent to registered mobile number within 5 minutes
- Order details accessible through customer account portal under "My Orders"

**Information Included in Confirmation:**
- Unique order number (format: TM-2024-XXXXXX)
- Itemized list of purchased products with images
- Total order value breakdown (subtotal, shipping, taxes, discounts)
- Delivery address as provided
- Estimated delivery timeframe
- Payment method and status
- Links to track order and contact customer service

**Timeline:** Order confirmation generated immediately upon successful checkout completion.

### STEP 2: Order Processing and Stock Verification

**Action:** TechMart warehouse team processes the order and verifies product availability.

**Processing Steps:**
- Order automatically routed to appropriate fulfillment center based on delivery address
- Inventory management system confirms product availability
- If product out of stock: Customer contacted within 4 hours with options (wait for restock, substitute product, partial shipment, or cancellation with full refund)
- For in-stock items: Order moves to picking and packing queue
- High-value orders (>10,000 EGP) undergo additional verification including payment confirmation callback

**Order Status Updates:**
- "Order Received" - Initial status after placement
- "Processing" - Under preparation at warehouse
- "Quality Check" - Products being inspected before packing
- "Packed" - Ready for courier pickup
- "Shipped" - Handed over to courier partner

**Timeline:** Standard processing completed within 12-24 hours for in-stock items. Orders placed after 4 PM may be processed the next business day.

### STEP 3: Professional Packing and Quality Assurance

**Action:** Warehouse team carefully packs products using appropriate materials and methods.

**Packing Standards:**
- Products remain in original manufacturer packaging
- Additional protective materials added: bubble wrap, foam inserts, air pillows
- Multiple items consolidated in single outer shipping box when possible
- Fragile items marked with "FRAGILE" stickers on all six sides
- Heavy items (>10 kg) packed in reinforced double-wall boxes
- Water-sensitive electronics sealed in plastic bags before boxing

**Quality Checks:**
- Physical inspection of product packaging for existing damage
- Verification of product model against order specifications
- Confirmation all listed accessories are included
- Serial number recorded in system for tracking purposes
- Weight verification to ensure completeness

**Documentation Included:**
- Packing slip with itemized list of contents
- Original invoice or tax receipt
- Product warranty cards and registration information
- User manuals and quick start guides (if not included in product box)
- Return instructions and TechMart contact information card

**Timeline:** Packing completed within 6-12 hours after order processing approval.

### STEP 4: Handover to Courier Partner and Tracking Activation

**Action:** Packed orders transferred to authorized courier partner for delivery.

**Courier Assignment Process:**
- System automatically assigns courier based on delivery zone and service level
- Courier collects packages from TechMart fulfillment center
- Each package receives unique tracking number (AWB - Air Waybill Number)
- Package information uploaded to courier's tracking system
- GPS tracking activated for real-time location monitoring

**Customer Notification:**
- "Shipped" status update SMS sent immediately
- Email with tracking number and courier information sent within 1 hour
- Estimated delivery date and time window provided
- Direct link to track shipment on courier's website
- Mobile app push notification (if app installed)

**Tracking Information Includes:**
- Courier company name and contact number
- Tracking/AWB number
- Current package location
- Estimated delivery date
- Delivery route milestones
- Expected delivery time window

**Timeline:** Courier pickup occurs within 4-8 hours after packing completion. Tracking information activated within 2 hours of courier collection.

### STEP 5: In-Transit Monitoring and Customer Updates

**Action:** Package moves through courier network toward destination with continuous monitoring.

**Transit Stages:**
- Origin facility scan (package leaves TechMart warehouse)
- In-transit to destination city/governorate
- Arrival at destination distribution center
- Out for delivery (loaded on delivery vehicle)
- Delivery area reached

**Automated Customer Notifications:**
- Package departed origin: SMS + Email
- Package arrived at destination city: SMS notification
- Out for delivery (same day): SMS with estimated arrival time window
- Driver assigned: SMS with driver name and contact number
- Package 30 minutes away: Final SMS alert

**Proactive Issue Management:**
- Delayed shipments automatically flagged in system
- Customer service team contacts customers proactively if delays expected
- Alternative delivery arrangements offered if needed
- Compensation process initiated automatically for eligible delays (see Policy TM-POL-003-2024)

**Customer Self-Service Options:**
- Real-time tracking via website, app, or SMS query (send tracking number to 16555)
- Delivery time rescheduling (up to 3 hours before scheduled delivery)
- Delivery instructions update (gate code, preferred location, etc.)
- Contact courier driver directly for coordination

**Timeline:** Transit times vary by zone (see Section 5). Standard Zone 1 deliveries: 24-48 hours total from shipment.

### STEP 6: Delivery Coordination and Customer Contact

**Action:** Courier driver contacts customer to coordinate final delivery.

**Pre-Delivery Communication:**
- Driver calls customer 30-60 minutes before arrival
- Confirms customer availability at delivery address
- Provides specific estimated arrival time (15-30 minute window)
- Confirms delivery location and any special instructions
- Alternative contact number called if customer doesn't answer first number (up to 3 call attempts)

**Customer Responsibilities:**
- Answer phone calls from courier (number provided in tracking notifications)
- Be available at delivery address during confirmed time window
- Provide building access codes or gate access if required
- Have cash ready for COD orders (exact amount or reasonable change)
- Prepare space for large item deliveries

**Delivery Scheduling Options:**
- Standard delivery: Between 9 AM - 6 PM
- Evening delivery: Between 6 PM - 9 PM (+30 EGP surcharge)
- Weekend delivery: Friday/Saturday (+50 EGP surcharge)
- Specific time slot request: 2-hour windows available upon request

**Timeline:** Courier attempts contact 30-60 minutes before delivery. If customer unreachable after 3 calls, delivery may be rescheduled to next day.

### STEP 7: Product Delivery and Handover

**Action:** Courier arrives at delivery address and completes product handover.

**Delivery Procedure:**
- Driver verifies delivery address matches order information
- Driver confirms recipient identity (checks ID if required)
- Driver presents packages for customer inspection
- Customer inspects exterior packaging for damage
- Driver waits while customer verifies number of packages and external condition
- Customer notes any damage on delivery receipt
- For COD orders: Customer makes payment, driver provides payment receipt

**Delivery Receipt Process:**
- Driver presents electronic device (tablet/phone) or paper receipt for signature
- Receipt includes: delivery date/time, recipient name, package condition notes, COD payment confirmation (if applicable)
- Customer signature indicates acceptance of delivery
- Customer receives copy of signed delivery receipt (photo emailed within 1 hour)

**Contactless Delivery Option:**
- Customer can request contactless delivery in order notes or via customer service
- Driver places package at specified location (doorstep, reception, etc.)
- Driver takes photograph showing package at delivery location
- Driver calls customer to confirm delivery completed
- Photo and delivery confirmation sent via SMS/email
- Note: Contactless delivery not available for COD orders or high-value items (>10,000 EGP)

**Installation Services (For Applicable Products):**
- Installation technician arrives within agreed time window (scheduled separately)
- Technician unpacks product and inspects for damage
- Professional installation performed per manufacturer specifications
- Functionality testing completed
- Debris and packaging materials removed
- Customer receives installation completion certificate and warranty activation

**Timeline:** Typical delivery handover takes 5-10 minutes for standard items, 30-120 minutes for products requiring installation.

### STEP 8: Post-Delivery Confirmation and Verification

**Action:** Customer verifies order completion and provides feedback.

**Immediate Post-Delivery:**
- Delivery confirmation SMS sent to customer within 15 minutes
- Email confirmation with delivery photo (if available) sent within 1 hour
- Order status updated to "Delivered" in customer account
- Delivery timestamp recorded for warranty and return period calculation

**Customer Verification Checklist:**
- Unbox product carefully and inspect for damage
- Verify product model, color, and specifications match order
- Check all accessories and components against checklist
- Test basic functionality (power on, initial setup)
- Register product warranty if applicable
- Report any issues within 48 hours for concealed damage claims

**Satisfaction Survey:**
- Automated survey sent via email 24 hours after delivery
- Rate delivery experience on scale of 1-5 stars
- Provide feedback on courier professionalism and delivery speed
- Option to upload photos for positive or negative feedback
- Responses used to improve delivery service quality

**Issue Reporting:**
If any problems discovered:
- Damaged product: Contact customer service at 16555 immediately, reference Return Policy (TM-POL-001-2024)
- Wrong product: Contact customer service within 24 hours for immediate exchange
- Missing items/accessories: Report within 48 hours with photographic evidence
- Installation issues: Contact technical support within 7 days

**Timeline:** Post-delivery verification should be completed within 48 hours of delivery to ensure full protection under TechMart policies.

### STEP 9: Warranty Registration and Documentation Storage

**Action:** Customer registers product warranty and maintains delivery documentation.

**Warranty Registration:**
- Register product through TechMart website: www.techmart-eg.com/warranty-registration
- Provide order number, product serial number, and delivery date
- Upload photo of product and purchase receipt
- Receive warranty confirmation certificate via email
- Warranty period begins from delivery date (not purchase date)

**Document Retention:**
- Keep original invoice/receipt for warranty claims and potential returns
- Save all delivery confirmation emails and SMS
- Store product serial number and warranty certificate securely
- Maintain installation certificates for applicable products
- Digital copies automatically stored in customer account portal

**Timeline:** Warranty registration should be completed within 30 days of delivery for optimal service.

### STEP 10: Delivery Case Closure and Loyalty Rewards

**Action:** TechMart closes delivery case and applies loyalty program benefits.

**Automatic Case Closure:**
- Delivery case automatically closed 7 days after successful delivery if no issues reported
- All delivery-related data archived in customer account history
- Transaction record finalized for accounting and inventory purposes

**Loyalty Points Crediting:**
- TechMart Rewards points credited to customer account
- Points calculation: 1 point per 10 EGP spent (excluding shipping fees)
- Bonus points for on-time deliveries and positive feedback
- Points redeemable for discounts on future purchases

**VIP Tier Progression:**
- Purchase amount counts toward VIP tier qualification
- Tier benefits: Gold (10,000 EGP annual spend), Platinum (25,000 EGP), Diamond (50,000 EGP)
- Tier benefits include free shipping, extended returns, priority support

**Timeline:** Loyalty points appear in customer account within 48-72 hours of case closure.
